# Code of Conduct

AgentPack should be a practical, respectful place to report issues, discuss
design trade-offs, and contribute fixes.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, specific, and respectful.
- Assume good intent, but correct problems with evidence.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the technical work.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds, experience levels, and tools.
- Credit prior work and avoid misrepresenting results, benchmarks, or claims.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic disruption after maintainers ask for focus.
- Bad-faith benchmark, security, or vulnerability claims.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns by opening a private security advisory when privacy is
needed, or by contacting the maintainers through the repository owner profile.
Include links, screenshots, or timestamps when available.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other
reasonable action to protect the project.
